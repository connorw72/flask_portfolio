Learn About Pet Adoption

In many places around San Diego, the rate of dogs ending up in shelters is increasing rapidly. This is due to many owners just leaving their dogs on the street once realizing the commitment it takes to care for them. This is leading to pet adoption centers growing out of proportion which is leading in many dogs becoming malnourished and unhealthy. The only way we can solve this problem is if families are willing to adopt some of these dogs and give them the care they deserve. There are multiple different sites that are open in San Diego where you can adopt a dog. The more popular locations to adopt dogs are the San Diego Humane Society and Helen Woodward Center.
